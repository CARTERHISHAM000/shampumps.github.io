About Sham Pumps

Sham Pumps is a professional company dealing in water pumping solutions and solar systems. We specialize in supplying, installing, and maintaining all types of water pumps and accessories. Our mission is to provide reliable, affordable, and long-lasting solutions tailored to customer needs.

Our Services

Pump Installation

Complete installation of submersible, surface, and centrifugal pumps for domestic, agricultural, and industrial use.

Solar Pump Systems

Design and installation of solar-powered water pumping systems for off-grid and hybrid setups.

Borehole Drilling

Professional borehole drilling services including hand-dug boreholes and mechanized drilling.

Maintenance & Repairs

Troubleshooting, servicing, and repair of pumps, controllers, and solar systems.
